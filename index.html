I am an Assistant Professor of Political Science at Columbia University, specializing in political psychology with a focus on attitude and belief change. My research examines how the quality of political information influences political engagement and representation, paying particular attention to the impact of misinformation within immigrant communities. My most recent work examines the potential for generative artificial intelligence to improve our understanding of public opinion.
